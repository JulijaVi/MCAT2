Media Content Analysis Tool

Does the article cite a person described as a ‘youth’ or ‘young person’ or include a citation from a person under 35 years of age, or is the topic about youth rights or issues?

Does the story actively include and positively highlight the experiences, opinions, and stories of minority groups and traditionally excluded sectors?

Does the format match any of these options: Reels, TikToks, short form articles, social media posts, or audiovisual content?

Does the primary topic of the story match any of these categories: Environment, social issues, human rights, inclusivity, health, reproductive rights, local politics?

Does the article clearly include a bias about the subject matter?

Is the topic of the article any of the following: women’s issues or rights, LGBTQ+ rights, rights of people with disabilities, ethnic or racial minority rights?

Does the text demonstrate gender bias by not using feminitives when appropriate? Does the text default to masculine pronouns when gender is unknown?
